About Us

Here at Ruthless Kustomz we truly have a passion and love for motorcycles. We take great pride in our workmanship and our customer service. Customer service and safety are our top priorities. We always keep our customers well informed with a step by step process of the actual work being performed on their motorcycles. We also provide pictures of all work being done on the client's vehicle. We make suggestions when we see potential problems with their motorcycles even when it is outside of the current project. With over 25 years of experience, I have personally been working on motorcycles since the age of 13. I also have been riding them since the age of 7. I always have built bikes for friends and myself. I even have done plenty of work for local shops in my area that need my service.

Our Goals

I recently decided that I would dedicate all my time and energy to building a new following for a customer base. I know that giving that honest, good old fashioned customer service which has been lost generations ago, will surely have individuals coming back for me. I base my business on amazing customer service good workmanship honesty and integrity. We never take on more work then we can handle. This not a volume based business, this is a business being built on true love and passion for motorcycles and the lifestyle that comes along with it. The greatest reward is when you have a customer that sees you as a friend after you have made their machine run to its peak performance and made sure their safety is top priority while doing it. Here at Ruthless Kustomz we can build your dream bike or make you a simple scooter. We have built really good relationships with the best vendors in the business. We try and provide our customers with many different options and ideas if they would like our professional opinion for their custom builds. I will be the first to say we are not the cheapest around but you will get the very best. That means 110% of everything we service for you. Remember this, you get what you pay for.

    • Our Services

    Repair & Servicing

    We work on motorcycles, dirt bikes, ATV/scooters, and watercrafts. Some of our services include: Tires, chain lube and adjustment.

    Tune Ups

    Our vast range of tune ups include: Brake Changes, fork seal replacements, oil changes, exhaust modifications, and suspension setup.

    Fuel Injection Cleaning

    We do a complete check on each fuel injector. All injectors are cleaned with Our UltraSonic machine that is set to our Asnu machine internal parameters set to your vehicles specific make and model.

    Pick Up Service

    If you ever have any issues with your bike or simply don't have a ride home after dropping off your ride for service, I offer a free pickup service that ranges 15 miles. After 15 miles, the only thing I charge is the fixed amount for gas. Please call ahead.

    Customization/Fabrication

    Custom lighting, custom builds, extended swing arms, custom paint, fairing repair, etc.

    Motor Rebuilds/Adjustments

    Carburetor jetting, tune ups & syncing, custom engine work, valve adjustment/replacement.
